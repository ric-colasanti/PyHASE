Langton's Ant


Rules

1. If the ant is at a WHITE cell, it turns RIGHT 90 degrees and changes the cell it is on to BLACK, then moves forward one cell as a unit.

2. If the ant is at a BLACK cell, it turns LEFT 90 degrees and changes the cell it is on to WHITE, then moves forward one cell as a unit
